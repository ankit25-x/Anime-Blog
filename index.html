Your Friendly Neighbourhood Anime Guide is Here!

Hello friends and fellow anime fans! I bring to you the ultimate guide to watch the best anime series that have released so far. So sit tight and keep reading through!

1. Naruto

One of the most popular and celebrated shows of all time Naruto and Naruto Shipudden have to talked about if you are talking about anime. Although some may say that the number of episodes is a herculean task, I would say that it is all worth the trouble! Naruto has 282 episodes which if one watches, makes it even more intriguing to watch the next 500!

2. Bleach

Started in the year 2004, this anime is one of the best storylines one can experience with the perfect villain arcs that will make you fall for it. Be wary of those filler episodes and you will have a lasting impression of this masterpiece! The number of episodes on this show is 366! Although if you skip filler arcs you may finish it much easily.

3. Dragonball Z

No anime discussion is complete without our very own Goku and Vegeta! The amount of episodes are boundless with respect to the level of fun and entertainment one can expeierence from this series. With Dragonball Z, Dragonball Super and Dragonball Heroes the list is endless. We all sincerely hope that Goku keeps on finding new power and the show never ends!. P.S - And Vegeta too!

4. Death Note

Another gripping series involving a book that spells death for people. Its a short series but you absolutely won't regret this choice. The perfect villain and the perfect mastermind are waiting for you! Its just 2 seasons 0f 24 episodes each but it really proves that to make something great you don't need to have super long seasons and sagas.

Well this was all contemporary, let me tell you more about some new concepts that have rocked the anime world!

1. Attack on Titan

The Sory revolves around a war between the human race and titans for survival. This thriller story is not only gripping, ruthless but also unpredictable - it will keep you guessing till the last second! It has a total of 4 seasons with a fifth season in the making!

2. Dr. Stone

Based on an entirely new concept this story stands in its own league. A story where the modern age has been swept back to the stone age where everyone has turned to stone by some mystical rays. This makes the story unpredictable and the viewer question his own thoughts. Spoiler Alert- Those love fundamental science and applications are going to love this. It has currently only one season with 24 episodes.

3. My Hero Academia

The story is themed around a world where all humans have some or the other quirks where a boy narrates how he became the most powerful hero on the planet! Need I say more? This series has a total of 5 seasons with fifth season currently running! Episodes air on link at the bottom on every Saturday and on Netflix every Monday.

4. Demon Slayer

This is my personal favourite. The best one yet. The story begins with the protagonist's family dead and her sister turned into a demon. Its about their journey together to find the person responsible and becoming strong together.A must watch! A single yet powerful season of 24 episodes and a follow up movie ought to keep you hooked till the last minute!

5. Jujutsu Kaisen

Another interesting story that is making its mark in the world of anime. Only the first season of 24 episodes is out but those 24 won't let you leave the screens till the end! The combination of curses and sorcerers certainly blend well together, question is do you agree? Available on netflix and other online portals.
